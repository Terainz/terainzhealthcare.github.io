V-Ca is a nutritious way of adding to the body’s supply of Vitamin C and Calcium. V-Ca drink is a perfect way to start your day.

Health Benefits of V-Ca:

1.    V-Ca facilitates calcium absorption, making it more bio-available to the cells.

2.    It aids the absorption of Iron, stimulation of immune system and also acts as an antioxidant to strengthen the immune system.

3.    It neutralizes potentially harmful reactions in the watery parts of the body, such as blood and fluids both inside and surrounding cells.

4.    V-Ca may be useful as an immune stimulator and modulator in some circumstances and promotes resistance to infections.
